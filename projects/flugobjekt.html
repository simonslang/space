FLUGOBJEKT

The production cycle is 35ms. Becoming a flying object seems to be a first step.

/////////////////////////

Year of production: 2024-2025

ESP32, Custom PCB, 20 magnetic valves, PU Tube, Water Basin

Movement generated by a noise map.

/////////////////////////

“Flugobjekt” is a generative light sculpture which explores acceleration phenomena and aliasing effects and aims to describe our media-loaded present.

Between Paul Virilio‘s dromology - the acceleration of society through media and the resulting overstimulation - and the philosophical movement of accelerationism - the escape from a capitalist system through acceleration and destabilization - “Flugobjekt” provokes to open a dialogue.

Data seems to float from a source of information. Ascended, the generated water droplets float in a chaotic order.

With technical force a deeply fragile simulation is created.

The continuous of the analogue world has become quantised and digitalised.

Within this fragmented reality the forces are in different order.

The sound of the restless, calculating solenoids merges with gently rippling water. The sound of a mechanical nature. We were living in a digital world all along.
