Irgendwo

is a self-managed cultural open space on a outdoor space near Bremen Airport. During the project period of 5 months, we host a mixture of parties, concerts, workshops, yoga, theater and cinema. The whole thing is open air, with two stages and a spacious area around it. We try to provide a low-threshold cultural offering that everyone should have access to, regardless of origin, gender, level of education, sexual orientation, financial means, age and other often marginalizing factors. The aim is to promote modern urban development that is shaped by its citizens.

I became part of Irgendwo in the summer of 2020 and since then i'm in charge of the light design. Furthrtmore i host my own events at the site and develop a sustainable infrastructure.
