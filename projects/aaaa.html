Angrezi Art Academy Award - The quadruple A

The quadruple A is the most prestigious art academy award presented annually by Radio Angrezi at the HfK Bremen in 5 glamorous categories:

Cheesy – For the work that embraces kitsch, sentimentality, or over-the-top drama in the most glorious way.

Angry – For the project that channels pure rage, critique, or raw emotional energy — whether through form, content, or execution.

Confusing – For the piece that leaves audiences puzzled, scratching their heads, or questioning reality itself.

Dangerous – For the artwork that pushes boundaries, teeters on the edge, or even poses a mild existential (or physical) threat.

L’art pour l’art – For the most unapologetically artistic project—something that exists purely for the sake of art, without justification or compromise.

The award show during the University Days in which the artworks were presented was live broadcasted on Radio Angrezi. After a very complicated public voting system with multiple fraud cases the winners were nominated in a live Award Ceremony. Even the radio itself was able to win in the category: L’art pour l’art.
