Light design projects

Untitled (Ballerrahmen)

Untitled (Ballerrahmen) is an immersive light installation. Constructed out of white colored pieces of bark and directional LEDs, the material melts before the viewer's eye and appears fluid. The sine wave motion of the lights causes the shadows to flicker and the certainty of the bark's nature to evaporate.

Designed and built together with Jens Mysliwietz.

Second Sun

"Second Sun" is a video mapped mirror ball. The targeted projection onto individual mirror surfaces and the beam-shaped reflection result in complex light sculptures.

Monitore

"Monitore" is a video installation at the Chaos Office. The project was built almost entirely from found materials within a few days. Besides my own cinematic experiments, the installation is regularly controlled by various VJs.
